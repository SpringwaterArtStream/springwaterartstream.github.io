About me

Painting by: Gerry Squires

Springwater Art Stream.

Bill Coultas began his video production career when he was hired by Cable Atlantic the local community television station. While there he produced numerous tv series, live sport and religious broadcasts and some documentaries netting him three "Award of Excellence" honours. After eight years with Cable Atlantic he founded his own independent television production company titles Springwater Productions Inc. Over a twenty year period he produced nine documentaries for regional, national and international broadcasters. His clients included local CBC and NTV stations, national clients - BRAVO! and CBC Newsworld. Internationally he produced a documentary for RFO France titled "Face au Soliel".

He also produced two seasons of 'Homegrown', a horticulture series along with three seasons of 'Breaking Ground' a series devoted to agriculture in Newfoundland and Labrador.
